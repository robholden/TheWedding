Accommodation

Marriott Hotel, Kochi

We have arranged a block of Deluxe rooms at the 5-star Marriott Hotel for selected guests for 4 nights. It is located only 30 minutes away from Cochin International Airport. The rooms come with: free buffet breakfast, free wifi, mini fridge & access to the swimming & fitness centre.

Check-in is on the 27th December at 3pm and check-out is on the 31st December at 12pm

Some of you might want to arrive earlier or stay back for a few days to explore and enjoy the beauty of Kerala. So, just let us know in the RSVP form if you would like your stays to be extended; but please be informed this will come as an additional cost to yourself.

Flight Information

Cochin International Airport

Please ensure your flights are booked to arrive at Cochin International Airport (COK).

We suggest you land on the 27th of December (check-in at 3pm) and depart after the 31st of December (check-out at 12pm). However, the first function is not until 2pm, 28th December; so if you need to arrive later you can — just let us know via the RSVP form.

Travel

Getting around Kochi

Our event planner will organise and arrange transport to all venues and functions.

We will have a Whatsapp group for all guests staying in the hotel — please provide your phone number in the RSVP form.

For getting around the city, you can use services like Uber, or the Kochi Metro. The hotel is conveniently located near a metro station, making it easy to explore the city.

If you want the true Kerala experience, make sure you take a ride in an auto rickshaw for short distances. They are a fun and affordable way to get around the city.
